# Current Main LPLH2 Version

Current main version as of 2026-07-18:

```text
versions/lplh2_room_identity_familiarity_patch_2026-07-18
```

This is the current thesis/testing baseline. Older folders under `versions/`
remain preserved as historical snapshots.

The main-version designation includes:

- grounded achievement, death, and reward-enabler memories;
- a persistent reward directory with exact scoring commands, setup commands,
  observed route hints, and current-epoch earned state;
- sparse, dynamic experience retrieval with exact physical-room relevance;
- storage and retrieval deduplication for equivalent route lessons;
- current-epoch suppression of already-earned achievements and linked enablers;
- affordance brainstorming with a per-location carryover agenda;
- compact known-reward, relevant-lesson, object-history, and tried-here context;
- persistent object interaction statistics with advisory futility notes;
- failed-command memory, attempt ledger, and same-state repetition memory;
- dedicated inventory reconciliation and KG/world-state updates;
- visit-scoped repeated-navigation enforcement;
- LLM-centered room identity, signature refinement, and visit-count advice;
- structural blocked/open contradiction splitting with arrival-edge identity
  tie-breaking and isolated per-room attempt memories;
- FRESH, COVERED, and EXHAUSTED familiarity labels for rooms, objects, and
  repeatedly unproductive commands;
- first-visit described-exit guidance and untouched-object brainstorming;
- persistent structured reward routes with next-hop guidance and recoverable
  hop-failure annotations;
- causal setup filtering that excludes movement and observation-only actions;
- source-room route retrieval, exact-room filtered reward-memory fetches, and
  explicit retrieval drop diagnostics;
- preserved `<< Room Title >>` identities and fingerprint-aware event keys;
- terminal defeat/victory detection and deterministic start-memory retrieval;
- detailed summary, retrieval, gate, action, KG, agenda, and timing logs;
- persistent room-level preparation goals with concrete inventory keywords,
  reversible confirmation, survival evidence, and merged death warnings.

The main version intentionally does not include active planning or forced BFS
navigation.
